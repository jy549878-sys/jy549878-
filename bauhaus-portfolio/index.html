About Me

I am a designer deeply influenced by the Bauhaus movement, emphasizing functionality, minimalism, and geometric purity in all my work.

With a background in both graphic and product design, I create solutions that are clean, precise, and purposeful, stripping away all unnecessary elements to reveal the essence of form and function.

My design philosophy centers around the Bauhaus principles of "form follows function," using bold typography, strong grid systems, and a restricted color palette to create visually striking yet highly functional designs.
